Description

Beef Tartare is a dish that uses super high quailty meat in its raw form and is marinated in a special sauce

Ingredients:

  • A5 Wagyu beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Capers
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Egg Yolks
  • Parsley
  • Kosher Salt

Steps:

  1. Chop the capers
  2. Freeze the beef
  3. Cut the beef into 1/4 inch slices
  4. Mix soy sauce, capers, and beef into a bowl
  5. Form into a mold
  6. Add egg on top
